Getting Started with Voice AI Writer

Welcome to the future of content creation! This guide will help you get the most out of Voice AI Writer's powerful features.

Setting Up Your Environment

1. API Configuration

Before you begin, you'll need to configure your AI API key:

  • Click the "Update API Key" button in the top right corner
  • Enter your Gemini AI API key
  • Your key is stored securely in your browser only

2. Microphone Permissions

Grant microphone access when prompted to enable voice recognition features.

Writing Your First Document

Step 1: Choose Your Content Type

Select what type of content you're creating from the dropdown menu:

  • Fiction
  • Blog Post
  • Academic Paper
  • Business Document
  • Creative Writing

Step 2: Start Speaking

Click the "Start Writing" button and begin speaking naturally. The AI will:

  • Transcribe your speech in real-time
  • Correct grammar and spelling automatically
  • Improve sentence structure and flow
  • Maintain your personal writing style

Step 3: Review and Export

Once you're finished speaking:

  • Review the AI-enhanced content
  • Make any final edits if needed
  • Copy to clipboard or download as a file

Tips for Best Results

Speaking Techniques

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Use natural pauses between sentences
  • Say "new paragraph" to create paragraph breaks
  • Speak punctuation when needed ("comma", "period", "question mark")

Content Structure

  • Start with an outline in your mind
  • Speak in complete thoughts
  • Use transitional phrases between ideas
  • Don't worry about perfect grammar - AI will fix it

Advanced Features

Voice Commands

Use these voice commands to control the writing process:

  • "New paragraph" - Creates a paragraph break
  • "New section" - Starts a new section
  • "Delete that" - Removes the last sentence
  • "Stop writing" - Pauses transcription

AI Enhancement Options

Customize how the AI processes your content:

  • Tone adjustment (formal, casual, academic)
  • Style preferences (concise, detailed, creative)
  • Target audience considerations

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Microphone not working: Check browser permissions and refresh the page
  • Poor transcription: Ensure you're in a quiet environment and speaking clearly
  • AI not responding: Verify your API key is correctly configured

Performance Tips

  • Use a good quality microphone for better accuracy
  • Minimize background noise
  • Take breaks during long writing sessions
  • Save your work frequently
